Portland Waldorf Model United Nations

We are the Portland Waldorf High School Model United Nations Club. Model United Nations, or MUN, is an extra-curricular activity in which our members role-play delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees at Annual Conferences. Each school's team assumes the role of a different country, with students diving deep into past policy decisions and geopolitical history to learn more about their country. MUN allows our members to experience and understand political and ethical positions other than their own, widening their worldview while developing their debate, critical thinking, and interpersonal skills.

If you have any interest in sponsoring the team, or would like to get in contact for any reason, please email general@pwhsmun.tk
