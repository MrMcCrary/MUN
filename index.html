Portland Waldorf Model United Nations

Portland Waldorf's Model UN club is proud to represent Portland Waldorf at confrences throught the year. Entirely student run, Portland Wladorf MUN focuses on the development of its member's public speaking and debating skills.

Founded in 2015 by Jackson Manwaring-Perks, the club has grown to include students from all grade levels and academic backgrounds.

If you have any interest in sponsoring the team, or would like to get in contact for any reason, please email general@pwhsmun.tk
